Why is my latitude and longitude invalid?
To input your latitude and longitude, simply key in the numbers accordingly! For latitude, your number must be between -90 to 90, while your latitude must be between -180 to 180! For your information, Singapore's latitude and longitude is 1.3521 and 103.8198 respectively!

Why is my address search not yielding accurate results?
When using the address function, do note that your input must be as precise as possible, and not just the main street name. For example, when searching for the nearest bus stop to Toa Payoh Swimming Complex, search for 'Toa Payoh Swimming Cplx' or 'Toa Payoh Swimming Complex', and not merely 'Toa Payoh'! If not, the search result may be inaccurate. Most probably, you will end up at Larkin Terminal, located all the way in Johor Bahru :), or if are luckier, Changi Beach.
